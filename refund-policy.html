Refund Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

1. Nature of Services

EastKode delivers custom professional services (consulting, system setup, integrations, and workflow implementation). Because work is tailored and labor-based, refunds are handled differently from product purchases.

2. Discovery and Planning Fees

Fees paid for discovery, auditing, strategy, planning, and architecture design are non-refundable once the session/work has started or deliverables have been shared.

3. Project Milestone Payments

  • Completed milestones are non-refundable once delivered and accepted, or once implementation work for that milestone has been executed.
  • If a milestone has not started, prepaid amounts for that milestone may be adjusted, credited, or refunded at EastKode's discretion and per contract terms.
  • If a project is paused due to missing client access/assets, active work time already spent remains billable and non-refundable.

4. Subscription / Retainer Support

Monthly support or retainer plans (if applicable) are billed for allocated service capacity and are generally non-refundable for the active billing cycle once started. Cancellation applies to future cycles unless otherwise agreed in writing.

5. Third-Party Costs

Third-party platform charges (hosting, API fees, WhatsApp provider charges, software subscriptions, etc.) are governed by those providers and are not refundable by EastKode.

6. Exceptional Cases

EastKode may provide partial refunds, service credits, or scope adjustments on a case-by-case basis where deemed fair and commercially reasonable.

7. How to Request Refund Review

Email hello@eastkode.in with project name, payment details, and reason for request. We review requests within a reasonable business timeframe and respond with the resolution path.

This refund policy is a standard professional-services policy template and should be reviewed by legal counsel before final contractual enforcement.
