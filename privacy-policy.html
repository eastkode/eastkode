Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

1. Who We Are

EastKode provides client communication and workflow automation services for small and medium businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit our website or engage our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit (such as name, email, business details, and message).
  • Communication details when you contact us via booking links, email, or forms.
  • Project-related operational data needed to deliver automations (for example workflow rules, CRM fields, and campaign setup inputs).
  • Basic technical data (such as browser/device information and website interaction metrics) where analytics is enabled.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide service proposals.
  • To design, build, deploy, monitor, and optimize client automation systems.
  • To provide support, maintenance, troubleshooting, and reporting.
  • To improve service quality, reliability, and security.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our contractual rights.

4. Data Ownership and Control

Client messaging data, customer lists, and business workflow data remain owned by the client. EastKode processes this information only to deliver contracted services and does not sell customer data or use it for unrelated purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We only share data with tools and providers required to deliver the service (for example hosting platforms, communication APIs, CRM integrations, or scheduling tools such as Calendly when you book a discovery call), and only to the extent necessary. We do not sell personal data to data brokers.

6. Security Measures

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including access controls, credential protection practices, and scoped operational access. No internet-based system can be guaranteed 100% secure, but we continuously work to reduce risk.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal and project information only as long as needed for service delivery, support, legal compliance, and legitimate business records. Upon termination, deletion/export workflows are handled according to project agreement and platform limits.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information we hold, subject to contractual and legal limitations.

9. Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact us at hello@eastkode.in.

This policy is a general business privacy policy template and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel for jurisdiction-specific compliance requirements.
